About Our School

Discover our rich history, mission, and the values that guide us in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Story

Utugi Junior Secondary School was established to serve the educational needs of the growing community in Nyeri County. What began as a humble learning centre has grown into one of the most respected junior secondary schools in the region.

With the transition to the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), our school embraced the new system wholeheartedly, investing in teacher training, modern facilities, and innovative learning approaches to prepare our learners for the demands of the 21st century.

Today, Utugi JSS stands as a beacon of academic excellence, with a track record of producing well-rounded learners who excel in academics, sports, and the arts.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in junior secondary education, producing competent, innovative, and value-driven learners who positively impact their communities.

Our Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and holistic education through the CBC framework, nurturing every learner's unique talents in a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment.

Our Motto

"Knowledge, Integrity, Excellence"

Our Core Values

The principles that define our school culture and guide every decision we make.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and moral uprightness in all our actions.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in teaching, learning, and all school activities.

Respect

We value every individual and treat all members of our community with dignity.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and new approaches to learning and problem-solving.

Our Goals

  • Achieve top performance in national junior secondary assessments
  • Develop competent, responsible, and patriotic citizens
  • Provide a safe and inclusive learning environment for all
  • Integrate technology across all learning areas
  • Excel in co-curricular activities at county and national levels
  • Foster strong partnerships with parents and the community
  • Promote environmental sustainability and conservation
  • Continuously improve school infrastructure and learning resources
