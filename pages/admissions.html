Admissions

Join the Utugi JSS family. Learn how to apply and give your child the gift of quality CBC education.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to enrol your child at Utugi Junior Secondary School.

1

Enquiry & Visit

Contact our admissions office or visit the school to learn more. We welcome school tours during weekdays from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

2

Obtain Application Form

Collect the admission form from the school office or download it below. Fill in all required details accurately.

3

Submit Documents

Submit the completed form together with required documents (see requirements below) to the admissions office.

4

Assessment & Interview

Learners undergo a brief assessment. Parents/guardians meet with the administration for a short interview.

5

Admission Confirmation

Successful applicants receive an admission letter. Pay the required fees and collect the uniform list to complete enrollment.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed admission application form
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Grade 6 leaving certificate / report card
  • NEMIS number confirmation
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Immunization records
  • Transfer letter (if from another school)

Eligibility

  • Completed Grade 6 under the CBC system
  • Age: Typically 12–15 years for Grade 7 entry
  • Placed by the Ministry of Education or direct application
  • Willingness to abide by school rules and regulations
  • Medical fitness (health form provided upon admission)
