Why Choose Utugi JSS?

We offer a nurturing environment where every learner thrives academically and personally.

CBC Excellence

Fully aligned with the Competency-Based Curriculum for holistic learner development.

Qualified Staff

TSC-registered teachers trained in CBC pedagogy and modern teaching methods.

Co-Curricular

Rich sports, music, drama, clubs and societies for all-round learner development.

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped classrooms, science labs, library, and computer room for quality learning.

CBC Curriculum

Preparing well-rounded learners ready for the challenges of the 21st century.

Competency-Based Learning

Focus on developing core competencies: communication, critical thinking, creativity, citizenship, collaboration, digital literacy, and self-efficacy.

STEM Integration

Hands-on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics experiences for all Grade 7-9 learners.

Arts & Sports Pathway

Creative arts, performing arts, and sports programmes that nurture talent and passion.

Values-Based Education

Strong emphasis on Kenyan values, patriotism, integrity, and community service.

From the Principal's Desk

Welcome to Our School

On behalf of the entire Utugi Junior Secondary School community, I warmly welcome you to our institution. Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing quality education that nurtures the whole child — intellectually, morally, socially, and physically.

Under the CBC framework, we strive to develop competent, confident young Kenyans who are ready to contribute positively to society. Our dedicated team of teachers works tirelessly to ensure every learner reaches their full potential.

We believe in partnerships with parents and the community to create the best learning outcomes. I invite you to visit us and experience the Utugi difference.

— Mr. John Mwangi, Principal
